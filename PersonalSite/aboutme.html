Why Computer Science?

In high school I took an online visual basic programming course being offered online to other students at my school. It was a work at your own pace class and the class had a proctor in the room but no real teacher. I worked ahead of schedule until I was finished with the 90 day class in about 45 days and first to finish the course. I ended up teaching the majority of the class on visual basic and helping in a way that the online teacher could not. My comprehension of programming was so different from the other students that I decided at the end of the course that I would go to school for Computer Science.
